Description: 
Smash Karts is a multiplayer online battle kart racing game. Players compete in fast-paced races where they not only race to be first but also use various weapons and power-ups to sabotage their opponents. The game features colorful, cartoonish graphics and a variety of karts and tracks. Players can upgrade their karts, collect power-ups, and engage in chaotic, action-packed races. The goal is to be the best racer and survive the mayhem while trying to finish in the top positions.
